# PWA Setup - Akses di iPhone/Android

## Apa itu PWA?
App seperti native app tapi dari browser. Bisa "Add to Home Screen" di iOS & Android.

---

## STEP 1: Deploy (Gunakan Salah Satu)

### Opsi A: Chrome Extension (Lokal)
- Hanya bisa di Chrome desktop
- Follow `EXTENSION_SETUP.md`

### Opsi B: Online (Bisa di iOS/Android)

**Pilihan:**
1. **Render** (FREE) - render.com
2. **Vercel** (FREE) - vercel.com
3. **Railway** (~$5/mo) - railway.app

**Paling mudah: Render**
- Sign up render.com
- Create Web Service
- Deploy (auto)
- Done!

---

## STEP 2: Akses di iPhone (iOS)

**Di iPhone Safari:**

1. Buka: `app-name.onrender.com` (atau domain deployment Anda)
2. Tap **"Share"** button (bottom)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Name: `POS System`
5. Tap **"Add"**
6. **Done!** Icon muncul di home screen

---

## STEP 3: Akses di Android

**Di Android Chrome:**

1. Buka: `app-name.onrender.com`
2. Tap **3 dots** (menu)
3. Tap **"Install app"** atau **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. **Done!** Icon muncul
